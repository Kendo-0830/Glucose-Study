Dual 2-Back Test

How It Works:

You'll see letters appear in different positions on a 3×3 grid. Your job is to remember both the position and letter from 2 steps ago.

When to Click:

  • Position: Current position matches 2 steps ago, but letter is different
  • Letter: Current letter matches 2 steps ago, but position is different
  • Both: Both position AND letter match 2 steps ago
  • Nothing: If neither matches, don't click

You have 30 trials. Each letter appears for 0.5 seconds, then disappears. You can click anytime during the 3-second trial window.
